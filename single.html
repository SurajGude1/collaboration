|| Embrace the technology rather than banning it ||

To understand Chatgpt, let us understand the basics of chatbot. Chatbot is a platform that gives instant answers to questions asked by users, with the help of artificial intelligence and Natural Language Processing. The chatbots which are available in the market are also called virtual assistance or virtual agents! Like when you visit the website of any company, you would have seen an icon like messenger, which provides assistance virtually!

In 1966 First chatbot was created by MIT computer scientist Joseph Weizenbaum whose name was ELIZA. Historically, the earlier chatbots used to answer only a limited set of queries. The main focus of the developers was to set only a limited set of queries. If the complexity of the queries was increased, then the earlier chatbots did not work so efficiently! But as innovation started happening in AI, advanced level chatbots started being created.

The initial version of chatgpt was released by San Francisco based company OpenAI on November 30, 2022! ChatGPT crossed 1 million users in just 5 days! ChatGPT became one of the fastest growing platform which completed 1 million users in just 5 days!

It is not that there were no AI chatbots before chatgpt and many chatbots were announced after the launch of ChatGPT! Now there are many alternatives to ChatGPT like: 1. Google Bard 2. Microsoft Bing Chat 3. Jasper.ai 4. Claude 5. ChatSonic 6. NeevaAI 7. YouChat 8. Perplexity 9. Character.AI 10. Elicit 11. Learnt.ai There are many chatbots like these that work on ai model and even Instagram has announced its own ai ChatBot!

Hope, all of you must have used chatgpt once, I have done it many times! When I was preparing for campus placement, I liked coding but I was not expert enough to solve complex or advance level problems, so we used to take help from those who knew coding well or sometimes from blackbox. Used to generate code. But after the advent of chatgpt, I have seen many such students who, if any problem is not being solved, are generating code directly from chatgpt with detailed solution. Students can learn coding comfortably without any help and companies' coding assessments too!

After the launch of ChagPT, many people told its positive and negative impact! Debates and new research studies also started happening on ChatGPT! To understand ChatGPT in depth, one such research case study was conducted by Ahmed Tlili and his team! The main focus of the study was how ChatGPT can bring revolution in education! To get the final conclusion, they analyzed the data in 3 ways! As 1.social network analysis of tweets 2.content analysis of interviews 3.investigation of user experiences Conclusion: Embrace the technology rather than banning it

Most important topic right now! What will happen to the jobs! Ever since chatgpt has been launched, people are feeling that their jobs are not in danger! Especially the people of Software filed!

May the innovation of engineers not become costly for the engineers themselves! Let's come to our main issue, whether automation of everything or introduction of new apps like ChatGPT will affect employment? If we look at the current ChatGPT, it is not capable enough to completely replace the jobs, jobs will definitely be reduced to some extent in the future! You have to think as an employee because if automation benefits the company, then they will definitely reduce the employees! Some companies have also integrated chatgpt to manage daily operations. And some companies have also forbidden employees to use chatgpt!

The sooner the one who adopts new technologies will remain in the market! For example, instead of hiring different frontend developer & backend developer, companies will hire only one full stack developer! Is ! It is not that the customers themselves start fulfilling the requirements by using chatgpt, the customers will first have to tell their requirements to the developer, the developer will convert those requirements into technical requirements and then go and prepare the final product. By the way, the developer is like a bridge between a customer and the final product!
